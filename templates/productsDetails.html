SELFSERV 83 RECYCLER

The NCR SelfServ™ 83 is an interior free standing pocket cash recycler with a contemporary look and feel and multi-touch capability. It is ideally suited for financial institutions looking to automate self-service for small business banking customers. Designed to perform with twice the reliability of other cash recycling solutions, the SelfServ 83 fulfils transactions with ease and convenience. The easy to use pocket interface is prominently positioned at the front of the ATM, delivering large bunch note capacity and improved visibility for consumer transactions. Advanced servicing capabilities ensure optimal performance and greater availability levels are achieved. Driven by NCR’s Media Handling 2.0 technology, the SelfServ 83 incorporates a new Bunch Recycling Module (BRM) delivering unrivalled performance, serviceability and security.
